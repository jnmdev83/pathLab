What does this test measure?

Total Cholesterol

The overall amount of cholesterol in your blood.

HDL (Good)

Helps remove extra cholesterol from your bloodstream.

LDL (Bad)

Main contributor to plaque buildup in arteries.

Triglycerides

Type of fat used for energy between meals.

VLDL

Carries triglycerides through your blood.

info Why it's important

Screening helps predict the risk of heart disease, stroke, and atherosclerosis. Early detection allows for lifestyle changes or medical intervention before complications occur.

warning Preparation

Requires 8-12 hours of overnight fasting. Water is permitted, but avoid alcohol and fatty meals 24 hours prior.
